Mental Health Assessment

Mental health is a key factor that affects students’ performance, focus, and ability to learn. Nursing students, in particular, face higher levels of stress due to academic demands and clinical training, making mental health care essential for their success.

Now, let’s take a moment to assess your mental well-being. Answer the following questions honestly.

1. How often do you feel emotionally drained?
2. Do you have trouble sleeping due to stress?
3. Is it hard to concentrate in clinical practice?
4. Do you feel physical pain (headaches) from stress?
5. Have you lost interest in nursing activities?
6. Do you feel overwhelmed by your future career?
7. Do you have time for your social life?
8. Do you avoid talking to friends?
9. Are you easily irritated?
10. Do you doubt your nursing skills?
